Common questions

Still have questions?

Whether you are interested in consulting, a design project, a healthcare partnership, or something else entirely — we are easy to reach and quick to respond.

Reach out directly
What does Nouvelle Ventures do?+
Nouvelle Ventures is an entrepreneurial holding ecosystem spanning interior design, healthcare, consulting, workforce solutions, and media. Each venture operates independently with a shared commitment to quality and intention.
How do I engage with Nouvelle Consulting Partners?+
Start with a discovery conversation. We focus on B2B companies navigating product marketing, positioning, and go-to-market execution. Use the contact form to get the conversation started.
Is McKinley Avenue Design Co. accepting new clients?+
We take on a limited number of design engagements per season. Submit an inquiry through the McKinley Avenue intake form and we will follow up within two business days.
Where is Newcrest Health currently operating?+
Newcrest Health is currently building toward launch in Texas. If you are interested in early partnership conversations, care staffing, or investor dialogue, reach out through our contact page.
How do I book a guest spot or sponsor the Black and White Podcast?+
Guest inquiries and sponsorship conversations can be initiated through our contact form. Include your concept or brand details and we will be in touch.
